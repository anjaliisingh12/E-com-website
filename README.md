## 🧠 How It Works

1. The homepage displays trending product cards.
2. Click on any card to open the **Product Details** view.
3. Choose quantity and click **Buy Now**.
4. A checkout popup opens asking for your details.
5. Enter your name, address, and phone number → Submit.
6. A confirmation alert is shown.
